Biography

Thom Whitehead is a freelance sound designer, audio engineer, and editor. He graduated with honors from the Savannah College of Art and Design with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Sound Design. Upon graduation, he worked at a recording studio in Philadelphia and two reputable post-production houses in New York City. He has worked on various multimedia projects as a freelance sound designer. His experiences include working on the sound for various forms of media, including film, video, and radio. He is also experienced in production sound booming and mixing, post production sound, dialogue, ADR recording and editing, 5.1 mixing, sound effects editing, music composition, sound design and video production & editing.

Recently, he has provided quality control for an audiobooks company and has provided post production sound design, mixing, and voice-overs for a variety of animated works. Past work experience has given him excellent communication and time management skills, as well as the ability to meet very strict deadlines.
